## How to Add a Learning
When Stark gives feedback ("too long", "wrong tone", "this worked well"):
- The executing agent appends a line to its section:
  `[2026-03-26] GOOD — captions with hooks outperformed plain intros — always open with a hook`
  `[2026-03-26] BAD — response was too long for a quick caption request — keep caption tasks under 5 lines`
- Next session, that agent reads its section before starting work

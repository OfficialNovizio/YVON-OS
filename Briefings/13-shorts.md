# 13 · Shorts (Distribution)

> Drop an edited short, and the copy + distribution gets prepared for every platform. A per-platform queue to push shorts everywhere.

## Purpose

"Post & schedule your shorts — drop one clip, get a card for every platform, manage each queue." The owner drops an edited short; agents prepare the copy and distribution for all social platforms; each platform has its own card/queue.

## Page header

- Title: **Shorts**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Shorts`.
- Sub-line: "Post & schedule your shorts — drop one clip, get a card for every platform, manage each queue."

## Upload / drop zone

- A large **"Drop a short here"** dropzone with constraints noted (e.g. "MP4/MOV or WebM · up to 1080p · captures for YouTube, Instagram & TikTok generated").
- **Choose file** button.
- A note that agents auto-generate platform captions from the dropped clip.

## Per-platform queue

- **Platform filter chips**: **YouTube**, **LinkedIn**, **Instagram**, **TikTok** (with avatars and per-platform status).
- A row of **platform cards** — one card per platform for the current short, each with its own preview, generated copy, and **Status: Ready** (or pending) so each queue can be managed independently.
- A note like "make sure they're per-platform" / "drag a card from the Short Pipeline" — i.e. cards arrive from the **Short Pipeline** (`12`).

## Workflow

Short Pipeline prepares clip → drop into Shorts → per-platform captions/cards generated → review/ready per platform → schedule (links into **Scheduler**, `15`) → distributed across YouTube/LinkedIn/Instagram/TikTok.

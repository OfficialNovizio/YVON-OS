# 19 · Asset Lab · Leonardo (Knowledge)

> The lab for the image-generation agent, **Leonardo**: every generated image in one place to view, download and reuse, plus **brand kits** (colors, logos, assets) per workspace.

## Purpose

"The lab for my image generation model." A gallery + workbench where the owner can see everything **Leonardo** has created, reuse/download assets, and define **brand kits** (color schemes, logos, assets) for each workspace so generated imagery stays on-brand.

## Page header

- Title: **Asset Lab**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Asset Lab · Leonardo`.
- Sub-line: "Leonardo is rendering X concepts · 1,2XX assets saved across your brands."
- Top-right toggles: **Gallery**, **Generate**, **Thumbnails** (modes/views).
- **Spend tracker** — cost figures for image generation, e.g. **$0.18 / $7.42 / $128.60** (last render / today / month), with a monthly budget note (e.g. "$129 / $200").

## Filters & search

- **Search assets** ("neon thumbnail," "human that flat lay," "Maria hero," …).
- **Brand filter chips**: **All brands**, **Vibe**, **By Design**, **Valhalla**, etc.
- **Type filter chips**: **All types**, **Thumbnail**, **Post**, **Hero**, **Cinematic**, **Graphic**, etc.

## Asset grid

- A masonry/grid of generated images, each tile showing the visual plus metadata: title (e.g. "Long-form thumb · big face, less text," "Cinematic Site hero — Maria project · cinematic dusk skyline," "The 5-asset stack"), the **brand/workspace**, **type**, a **cost** (e.g. $1.7/$1.2), and a relative time.
- Tile actions: **download**, **reuse**, and inspect what Leonardo was working with.

## Brand Kit (right rail)

- **Brand Kit** panel scoped to a workspace ("All brands" / per-brand):
  - **Display / style** settings (e.g. "Space Mood").
  - **Ratio** presets.
  - **Personas / brand persona** assets (e.g. "Nina · brand persona") used to keep generated humans consistent.
  - **Colors, logos, assets** for each workspace's brand identity.

## Agent & integrations

- **Leonardo** is the image-generation agent; outputs here feed **Social Approvals** (the 8 candidate images per post), **YouTube Studio** (thumbnails), **Cinematic Sites** (hero imagery), etc.
- Cost/budget tracking is built in so image-gen spend stays controlled.

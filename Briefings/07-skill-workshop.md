# 07 · Skill Workshop

> Where the agents get better. The "workshop & skill training" layer that continuously improves the master agents.

## Purpose

A dedicated space for training and improving agent skills. In the fleet, the third Mac mini is the "Workshop & Services" machine whose whole job is to **make the team better**; in the Org Chart, the bottom band is a row of per-master **Workshops** that constantly improve the master agents above them. This page is the front-end to that improvement loop.

## What it covers (from Org Chart + fleet context)

- **Per-master workshops** — e.g. *Mr. X's Workshop*, *William's Workshop*, *Leonardo's Workshop* — each focused on improving one master agent's capabilities (better copy, better image generation, better research, etc.).
- **Skill training / iteration** — building, testing, and refining the skills (tools, prompts, routines) that master agents use, then promoting improvements up to the live masters.
- Runs on the dedicated **Workshop & Services** machine so training load is isolated from production work.

## Position in the system

- Sits at the **top of the sidebar** (a core/command item) and at the **bottom of the Org Chart** (the improvement substrate).
- Feeds improvements into the **Workspace Tier** master agents (William, Leonardo, Isaac, etc.) that serve every workspace.

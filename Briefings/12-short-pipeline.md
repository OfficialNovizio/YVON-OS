# 12 · Short Pipeline (Shorts)

> The production flow for short-form clips — the Kanban equivalent of the Content Pipeline, but for Shorts, feeding the Shorts distribution page.

## Purpose

Move short-form content from idea/clip through to ready-to-distribute. Where the Content Pipeline handles long-form, the Short Pipeline manages the lifecycle of shorts (often derived from long-form videos, e.g. "follow-up Shorts" generated in YouTube Studio).

## What it covers (inferred from context + sibling screens)

- A staged board (idea → cut/clip → caption/copy → ready) for short clips.
- Cards feed the **Shorts** page (`13`) where the finished clip is uploaded and distributed to each platform.
- Connects to **YouTube Studio**, which can spin off follow-up Shorts from a long-form transcript.

## Relationship to other screens

- Upstream of **Shorts** (`13`) — the Short Pipeline prepares; the Shorts page distributes.
- Parallel in structure to **Content Pipeline** (`08`).

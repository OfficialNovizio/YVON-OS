# 09 · Production Calendar (Long-form)

> The date-based view of the long-form content machine — when each video is scripted, filmed, edited, and published, so cadence is visible at a glance.

## Purpose

A calendar layout over the Content Pipeline so the owner can see content scheduled across days/weeks and confirm the publishing cadence (e.g. 1 video/week) is being met. It is the "Calendar" counterpart to the Content Pipeline's Kanban.

## What it shows (inferred from context)

- A weekly/monthly grid with content cards placed on their target dates (filming days, edit days, publish dates).
- Each entry tied to a Content Pipeline card and its stage (Scripting → Filming → Editing → Ready → Published).
- Cadence/health at a glance: gaps in the schedule are visible, so the owner can keep the funnel full.

- Distinct from **Scheduler** (`15`), which schedules *social posts* across platforms; the Production Calendar is about *long-form video production*.

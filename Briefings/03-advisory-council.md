# 03 · Advisory Council (+ War Room)

> The owner's "board of strategic advisors" — five distinct agents with full memory access who debate, recommend, and can be convened into a live **War Room** session. The closing showcase of the walkthrough.

## Purpose

In the old system the advisory council under-performed because the advisor agents only had narrow knowledge of a few projects. With the new memory system, **five distinct agents with different roles get complete knowledge-base access.** They produce recommendations, debate topics, surface the patterns of what the owner keeps thinking about, and can be turned into an audio "podcast" to listen to (each agent gets a distinct **HeyGen** voice).

## Page header

- Title: **Advisory Council**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Advisory Council`.
- Buttons: **Set today's topic** and **Run a live session** (opens the War Room).
- Tabs/sections: **Recommendations**, **Outcomes / Review**, **Strategic Archive**.

## Today's recommendation (hero)

- A headline recommendation, e.g. *"Productize the agent-as-a-service offer now — turn the 'I run your agents' service into a fixed €2k/mo retainer, before the category name consolidates."*
- A **proposed-by** line crediting the agent(s) (e.g. a "needs your call / review" badge).
- A **First step** suggestion (concrete next action), e.g. "Package the Maria engagement as the template … and pitch it to your two warmest leads this week."
- An **audio player** — a waveform/podcast-style player (e.g. ~4:53) so the debate can be *listened* to rather than read. The five council agents appear as avatars under the player (Henry, Nexus, Ivy, By Design, Strategist, etc.) each contributing a voice.
- **Action buttons on the recommendation:**
  - **Accept** the proposal
  - **Accept & create task** (push it to the Task Board / assign)
  - **Assign to agent** (delegate acting on the finding)
  - **Ask follow-up** (question the council)
  - **Defer** / **Reject**

## Recommendations list

Below the hero, a stack of further recommendations (e.g. "Slow down the app-factory bet — prove By Design's retention before building #2"), each tagged (Product, Strategy, Finance…), credited to one or more council agents, with the same accept/assign/ask actions.

## Pattern Tracker (right rail)

- **Pattern Tracker** — surfaces recurring themes the council keeps raising ("Productize, stop doing bespoke," "Focus → fewer bets, deeper," "Repackage, don't re-create," "Get Wolf to put finance on it…"). This acts as a **mirror** showing the owner which topics are most urgent/most discussed.
- **"This month"** — a count/summary of topics discussed this period.

## Setting topics & live sessions

- **Set today's topic / set a topic for a particular day** — the owner can queue a specific question for which they want strategic input, framed as a debate.
- **Run a live session** — launches the **War Room** (below).

## War Room (live session modal)

A full-screen, cinematic live strategy room (a rendered 3D conference room — a cliffside cave with a round table and a city/ocean view at dusk).

- **Header:** `WAR ROOM`, a "Live session" indicator, **Pause** and **Skip** controls, the debate topic (e.g. *"Should I raise consulting rates 20% for new leads?"*), and a **LIVE** badge.
- **Left transcript rail:** a running live chat of the debate — each agent (You, Strategist, Nexus, …) speaks in turn with timestamped lines, arguing different angles (the data backs it / comparable founders charge more / counter-arguments about timing, etc.).
- **Center stage:** the 3D room with agent avatars positioned around the table (color-coded markers), and the currently-speaking agent's line captioned at the bottom.
- **The owner can jump in** — an input box ("Jump in — steer the debate…") to ask questions and steer the strategy in real time.
- **Council recommendation popup:** when the debate concludes, a card slides up — **"THE COUNCIL RECOMMENDS"** with the synthesized recommendation (e.g. *"Commit now — but with a hard 30-day checkpoint and a defined off-ramp. Capture the upside without betting blind."*). Actions: **Accept**, **Accept & create task**, **Back to Council**.

## Agents & voices (design intent)

Five role-distinct advisor agents, each with full knowledge-base access and a unique HeyGen voice, briefed heavily on what kinds of recommendations/topics they should cover. The output doubles as a listenable podcast.

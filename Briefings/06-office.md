# 06 · Office

> A playful 3D "office space" where the agents live — a visualization of what each agent is doing right now. The owner calls it "a bit of a gimmick, but fun," and a way to make the agent team tangible.

## Purpose

Turn abstract agent activity into something visual and tangible: a 3D rendered office where each agent has a desk, and you can see whether they're working, in a meeting, or idle, per workspace.

## Page header

- Title: **The Office**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Office`.
- A tagline: "Your agents, live on the floor — a room per workspace, a desk per agent, and they idle across rooms. Rest aside, never here."
- **Workspace tabs** to switch the floor: **Whole floor**, **Vibe**, **By Design**, **Valhalla** (one room per workspace).
- **Status legend / counters** top-right: **X working**, **X in a meeting**, **X cooling/idle**.

## The 3D scene

- An isometric/3D rendered open-plan office floor divided into **rooms (one per workspace)**, with **desks (one per agent)**.
- Agent avatars are placed at desks; their pose/animation reflects state — **at a desk working**, **in a meeting** (gathered around a table), or **idle/cooling**.
- The view is navigable (the floor can be explored; camera can move around the scene).
- Hovering/clicking a desk identifies the agent and what they're doing.

## Controls (bottom)

- Scene controls and a **"Scroll/zoom to look"** hint.
- A **"Speak to a [room/agent]"** action to jump into talking with an agent from the floor.

## Design intent

Primarily a tangibility/delight feature, explicitly flagged as a gimmick that will be **built out and improved over time** into a richer 3D world where the agents "live." It complements the data-dense Agents/Org Chart pages with an at-a-glance, human-feeling status view.

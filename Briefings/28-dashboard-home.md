# 28 · Dashboard / Home (System)

> The landing/overview screen of Mission Control. Appears as `dashboard-home.md` in the creator's own Briefings folder (screenshot 181510).

## Purpose

The default landing view when you open a workspace's Mission Control — a top-level overview that orients the owner before they dive into the Decision Queue. Where the **Decision Queue** is the action funnel ("the 7 things that need me"), the Dashboard/Home is the at-a-glance status of the whole operation.

## What it covers (inferred)

- A morning overview: system health, agents live, what was handled overnight, and a pointer into the Decision Queue.
- Summary tiles likely pulling from across the app: pending decisions, posts awaiting approval, tasks in review, software items needing review, inbox state, revenue/consulting status.
- Per-workspace, so it re-themes and re-scopes when you switch workspaces.

## Relationship to other screens

- Sits above the Command Center group; feeds the owner into **Decision Queue** (`01`), **Task Board** (`02`), and the analytics surfaces.
- Conceptually the "home" that the global top bar's status indicators (System healthy, agents live) summarize.

# 15 · Scheduler (Posts)

> The drag-and-drop calendar where approved posts land. Place posts across days/weeks to keep every platform's posting frequency fully active.

## Purpose

"Schedule across every platform … a card → a slot → publishing operations center on a calendar." A powerful drag-and-drop posting calendar so the owner can ensure their **posting frequency is met across all platforms**. Approved posts (from Social Approvals/Shorts) show up here and can be dragged into day/time slots.

## Page header

- Title: **Scheduler**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Scheduler`.
- Sub-line: "Schedule across every platform … publishing operations center."
- **Platform filter chips**: **All platforms**, **YouTube**, **LinkedIn**, **Instagram**, **TikTok**.
- Buttons: **Refine** and **Schedule next slot**.

## Calendar grid

- A **week/day calendar** (columns are days, e.g. 18–24) populated with **post cards** in time slots.
- Cards show the platform, the post (e.g. "Convert the agent trend," "Shipping software in 20 min," "Memory system breakdown") and time.
- **Drag and drop** to move a post to a different day/slot — the core interaction — so the schedule can be balanced and gaps filled to meet frequency targets.

## Right rail

- **Failure triage** — surfaces posts that failed to publish (e.g. "A post that didn't go: needs the API auth … re-auth"), with **Diagnose** and **Create fix task** actions, and a **Reauthorize** button. Keeps the posting engine healthy.
- **Publishing engine** — status of the auto-publishing system, connected accounts, next scheduled post, and queue health.

## Workflow

Social Approvals / Shorts "Schedule" → card appears in Scheduler → owner drags into the desired day/time → publishing engine posts automatically at the slot → failures surface in Failure triage for a quick fix → performance flows to **Social Analytics** (`16`).

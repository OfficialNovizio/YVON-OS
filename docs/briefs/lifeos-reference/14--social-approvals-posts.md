# 14 · Social Approvals (Posts)

> Everything regarding posts: for each post idea, Leonardo generates 8 images and William drafts A/B copy; the owner clicks an image and posts now or schedules. Sidebar badge: 6 pending.

## Purpose

The approval surface for social posts. For each post, the system presents generated imagery and copy variants so the owner just **picks and approves** — then posts immediately or sends it to the Scheduler. This is one of the main feeders of the Decision Queue (the "approve all three LinkedIn posts" type items).

## Page header

- Title: **Social Approvals**, breadcrumb `Vibe with AI / Social Approvals`.
- A "X of Y in the queue" progress indicator and **Review each** / batch controls.
- Top bar: **System healthy**, **agents live**.
- Sidebar badge shows the pending count (**6**).

## The post under review

- **Post context header** — e.g. *"Instagram post · from 'Claude ran my business'"* with a "1 of 3 in the experiment" indicator and a "Leonardo generated 8 images" note.
- **Copy variants (A/B)** — two text columns prepared by **William** (copywriting agent):
  - **Variant A** and **Variant B**, each the full caption (e.g. "I gave one business to a team of AI agents for 7 days. Here's exactly what shipped, what broke…"), so the owner can choose the stronger hook. Marked **recommended** on one.
- **Image grid** — **8 generated images from Leonardo** in a grid; the owner **clicks an image** to select it for the post. Includes an **"Upload your own image"** option.

## Actions

- **Post now** — publish immediately to the platform.
- **Schedule** — push to the **Scheduler** (`15`) to place on the posting calendar.
- Approve/skip controls to move through the queue; batch approve (e.g. approve all three in an experiment).

## Agents involved

- **Leonardo** — generates the 8 candidate images (Asset Lab).
- **William** — drafts the A/B caption variants.
- Henry escalates pending approvals into the **Decision Queue**.

## Workflow

Post idea → Leonardo (8 images) + William (A/B copy) → owner picks image + copy → **Post now** or **Schedule** → appears on the Scheduler calendar and later in Social Analytics.

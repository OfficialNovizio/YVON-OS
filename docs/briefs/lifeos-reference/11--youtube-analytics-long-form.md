# 11 · YouTube Analytics (Long-form)

> Performance view for the long-form channel — how published videos are doing, feeding back into ideas and titling.

## Purpose

Track long-form video performance (views, retention, CTR, subscriber impact) so the content engine learns what works and feeds those learnings back into the Content Pipeline (ideas) and YouTube Studio (titles/thumbnails).

## What it covers (inferred)

- Per-video and channel-level metrics: views, watch time/retention, click-through rate on thumbnails/titles, subscribers gained.
- Trends over time and comparisons between videos (which title/thumbnail patterns win) — consistent with Henry's data-informed title coaching in YouTube Studio.
- Insights that loop back into **Content Pipeline** ideas and **Trend Radar** content recommendations.

## Relationship to other screens

- Downstream of **YouTube Studio** (`10`) and **Content Pipeline** (`08`).
- Complements **Social Analytics** (`16`), which covers posts across social platforms rather than long-form YouTube.

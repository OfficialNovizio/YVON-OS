# Privacy Policy Builder

When this skill activates, build or audit a privacy policy. Must comply with GDPR (Art. 13/14), CCPA, CalOPPA, and COPPA where applicable.

## Required Disclosures (GDPR Art. 13)

### When Collecting Directly From Data Subject
1. Controller identity and contact details
2. DPO contact (if applicable)
3. Purposes of processing and lawful basis
4. Legitimate interests pursued (if basis used)
5. Recipients or categories of recipients
6. International transfers and safeguards (Art. 44-49)
7. Retention period or criteria to determine it
8. Rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection
9. Right to withdraw consent (if consent-based)
10. Right to lodge complaint with supervisory authority
11. Whether provision is statutory/contractual requirement
12. Automated decision-making logic and consequences (Art. 22)

### When NOT Collected From Data Subject (Art. 14)
All above PLUS:
13. Categories of personal data concerned
14. Source of personal data (publicly accessible or third party)

## CCPA/CPRA Disclosures (§1798.100)
- Categories of personal information collected (past 12 months)
- Categories of sources
- Business/commercial purpose for collection
- Categories of third parties with whom shared
- Specific pieces of personal information (on verifiable request)
- Categories sold or shared (and opt-out mechanism)
- Sensitive personal information categories (and limit use right)
- Retention period for each category

## Cookie Policy Requirements
Per ePrivacy Directive (EU) and GDPR:
- List all cookies by category: Necessary, Functional, Analytics, Advertising
- Purpose of each cookie
- Duration (session vs persistent)
- First-party vs third-party
- How to manage/disable
- Consent mechanism (cookie banner before non-essential cookies set)

## Children's Privacy (COPPA, GDPR Art. 8)
- If service targets children under 13: COPPA compliance required
- GDPR: children under 16 can't consent (member states can lower to 13)
- Must describe: what data collected from children, parental consent mechanism, parental rights

## Privacy Policy Audit Checklist
- [ ] Controller identity and contact
- [ ] ALL purposes of processing listed
- [ ] Lawful basis for EACH purpose
- [ ] Data retention periods (not vague "as long as necessary")
- [ ] All third-party recipients named
- [ ] International transfer safeguards described
- [ ] All 8 data subject rights explained
- [ ] How to exercise rights (email, form, phone)
- [ ] Complaint procedure (supervisory authority named)
- [ ] Cookie policy included or linked
- [ ] Last updated date
- [ ] Change notification policy
- [ ] Plain language — no legalese without explanation

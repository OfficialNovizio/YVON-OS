# GDPR Compliance Framework

The General Data Protection Regulation (EU 2016/679) applies to any organization processing personal data of EU residents, regardless of where the organization is based.

## Core Principles (Art. 5)
1. **Lawful, fair, transparent** — Clear legal basis, no deception
2. **Purpose limitation** — Specified, explicit, legitimate purposes
3. **Data minimization** — Adequate, relevant, limited to what is necessary
4. **Accuracy** — Kept up to date, erase/rectify without delay
5. **Storage limitation** — Kept no longer than necessary
6. **Integrity and confidentiality** — Appropriate security
7. **Accountability** — Controller responsible for demonstrating compliance

## Lawful Bases (Art. 6)
Processing is lawful only if ONE applies:
- **(a) Consent** — Freely given, specific, informed, unambiguous, withdrawable
- **(b) Contract** — Necessary for contract performance
- **(c) Legal obligation** — Required by law
- **(d) Vital interests** — Protecting life
- **(e) Public task** — Public interest
- **(f) Legitimate interests** — Requires LIA (Legitimate Interest Assessment)

## Data Subject Rights (Arts. 12-23)
| Right | Article | Response | Key Detail |
|-------|---------|----------|------------|
| Right to be informed | 13, 14 | At collection | Privacy notice required |
| Right of access | 15 | 1 month | Confirm processing, provide copy |
| Right to rectification | 16 | 1 month | Correct inaccurate, complete incomplete |
| Right to erasure | 17 | 1 month | "Right to be forgotten" — limited exceptions |
| Right to restrict processing | 18 | 1 month | While verifying accuracy |
| Right to data portability | 20 | 1 month | Machine-readable format |
| Right to object | 21 | 1 month | Including direct marketing (absolute) |
| Automated decision-making | 22 | 1 month | Including profiling |

## Breach Notification (Arts. 33-34)
- **To supervisory authority:** Within **72 hours** of becoming aware
- **To data subjects:** Without undue delay if high risk
- **Documentation:** Internal breach register mandatory

## Fines (Art. 83)
- **Lower tier (up to €10M or 2%):** Technical violations
- **Higher tier (up to €20M or 4%):** Principle violations
- Fines assessed per infringement — stacking possible

## Compliance Checklist
- [ ] Lawful basis identified and documented
- [ ] Privacy notice provided (Art. 13/14)
- [ ] Data minimization applied
- [ ] Retention period defined
- [ ] Security measures appropriate (Art. 32)
- [ ] DPA in place with all processors (Art. 28)
- [ ] Data subject rights process documented
- [ ] Breach notification procedure ready
- [ ] ROPA maintained (Art. 30)
- [ ] DPIA conducted if high risk (Art. 35)

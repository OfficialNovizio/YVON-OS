# Trademark Protection

When this skill activates, protect YVON intellectual property through trademark monitoring, registration guidance, and infringement detection.

## Trademark Basics

### Symbols
| Symbol | Meaning | When to Use |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **™** | Unregistered trademark claim | Anytime — no registration required |
| **℠** | Unregistered service mark | Same as ™ but for services |
| **®** | Federally registered trademark | ONLY after USPTO registration certificate issued |

**CRITICAL:** Using ® before registration is fraud. Can result in cancellation of application.

## Registration Process (USPTO)

### Timeline
```
Month 0: File application ($250-$350 per class for TEAS Plus)
Months 3-4: Assigned to examining attorney
Months 4-8: Office action or approval
Month 8: Publication for opposition (30 days)
Months 9-12: Notice of Allowance (intent-to-use) or Registration (use-based)
```
**Total:** 9-12 months typical. Expedited: 3-4 months (additional fee).

### Requirements
- **Use in commerce:** Must show specimen (screenshot of product/service using mark)
- **Distinctiveness spectrum** (strongest to weakest):
  1. **Fanciful** (Kodak, Xerox) — invented words — STRONGEST
  2. **Arbitrary** (Apple for computers) — real word, no relation to product
  3. **Suggestive** (Netflix) — suggests quality without describing
  4. **Descriptive** (Computer Store) — WEAK, needs secondary meaning
  5. **Generic** (Computer for computers) — NEVER registrable

| Check | Frequency | Method |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| **USPTO TESS database** | Monthly | Search for confusingly similar marks |
| **WIPO Global Brand DB** | Quarterly | International monitoring |
| **Domain registrations** | Monthly | Typosquatting, similar domains |
| **Social media handles** | Quarterly | Brand impersonation accounts |
| **App Store / Google Play** | Monthly | Clone apps using brand name |
| **Google Alerts** | Continuous | Brand mention monitoring |

## Enforcement Ladder

| Step | Action | When |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Monitoring | Always |
| 2 | Cease and desist letter | Clear infringement, no response needed yet |
| 3 | UDRP complaint | Domain cybersquatting |
| 4 | Trademark opposition | Confusingly similar application at USPTO (30-day window) |
| 5 | Litigation | Willful infringement causing damages |

## International Protection (WIPO Madrid Protocol)
- Single application covering 130+ countries
- Must have home country registration first
- Cost: CHF 653 base + per-country designation fees
- Renewal: Every 10 years
